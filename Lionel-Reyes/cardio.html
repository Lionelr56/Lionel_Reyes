Cardio



Cardio is mostly used as a way to lose weight but another benefit for it is it trains your heart and makes your arteries branch out therefore making your body more efficient at converting oxygen into carbon dioxide.



There is HIIT and it stands for High Intensity Interval Training and as the name states you do a cardio activity with extreme intensity and typically you do it in really short amounts of time. This is why this type is used by people that are short on time.



The other is called LISS and it stands for Low Intensity Steady State and this one is what most people think of when they think of cardio. You do an activity that can be sustain at a moderate pace for longer periods of time. Most bodybuilders would typically go for this of kind cardio. These are a couple of activies that are best for LISS cardio: jogging, cycling, swimming, boxing and even walking.
